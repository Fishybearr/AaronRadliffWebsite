PROJECTS

All of my projects largely vary in complexity and scale. Some of these I made before I knew anything about data structures, code formatting, or even that there were other languages than c#. I keep most of my old projects on here as I believe the most important part of programming is understanding and appreciating how far you've come. That being said, I know most people do not want to sift through the code I wrote when I was 14. So projects that are not up to my current standards get moved to Toys&Tools . This cuts down on clutter while allowing me to keep alive the projects that got me here today

TOYS & TOOLS

As stated above, Toys & Tools are small or old projects that aren't up to my standards to qualify as a project. Most of these are small webpages that I made to solve a very specific task. Things like the GPA Calculator I still use pretty regularly. To summarize, enjoy these at your own risk
